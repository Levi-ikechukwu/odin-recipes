Sphagetti Jollof Recipe

This is recipe is specially made by our able cheffs for Odins Project

INGREDIENTS

  • Water
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Coconut Oil
  • Raw Sphag

Steps

  1. Boil your water
  2. Wash the raw tomatoes
  3. Put your Raw sphagetti inside the boiled water
  4. Allow it to boil and get soft
  5. Put your tomatoes and ingredients
  6. Stir and cover
